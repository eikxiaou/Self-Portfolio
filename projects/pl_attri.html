P&L Attribution

  • PNL attribution service is the one stop shop for generating insightful reports out of daily attribution data.
  • SUPPORTED REPORTS
    • [1] Stat summary Gives insight over different statistics related to returns of the portfolio/strategies.
    [2] Group summary Breakdowns the returns into different groups, eg: Region, Country etc., allowing the user to attribute the returns obtained to different categories.
    [3] Strategy summary Breakdowns the returns into specific strategies.
    [4] Factor summary Breakdowns the returns into factors from the risk models.
